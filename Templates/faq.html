Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

A Hospital Management System is a software platform designed to manage the day-to-day operations and administrative tasks of a healthcare facility. This includes managing patient data, appointment scheduling, billing and payments, and inventory management.

The Hospital Management System can be used by healthcare providers, hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare facilities. It is designed to be used by both administrative staff and medical personnel.

Some of the benefits of using a Hospital Management System include improved patient care, increased efficiency and productivity, better communication between staff members, reduced errors and redundancies, and improved financial management.

Technical Questions

The Hospital Management System is built using a variety of technologies, including but not limited to: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Django, MySQL, and AWS.
